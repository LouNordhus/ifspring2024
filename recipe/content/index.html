Description:

rainbow sherbert is a frozen dessert made from water, sugar, a dairy product like milk or cream, and fruit flavoring.

Recipe Information

  • Allergens: dairy
  • Prep Time: 5hrs (making, chilling, and freezing the sherbert base)
  • Cooking time : 1 hour (making individual flavors out of bases, freezing them, combining them, then freezing again)
  • Serves: about 5 people
  • Equipment needed: piping bags, strainer, freezer safe containers,bowls,cling wrap, blender, spatula, saucepan

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/3 cup lime juice
  • 6oz fresh raspberries
  • orange food coloring
  • green food coloring

recipe source: bakedbyrachel.com

Instructions:

  1. combine milk, sugar, cream, and salt on medium heat on the stovetop. Then put mixture in refridgerator overnight
  2. Puree the raspberries and chill the mixture in the fridge before use
  3. divide semi-frozen ice cream base into thirds. Fold in puree and juice to create 3 colors and flavors
  4. add food coloring
  5. put mixtures into piping bags and freeze for 45 minutes
  6. snip off end of each piping bag and alternate piping out each flavour into a freezer safe container
  7. freeze until firm
